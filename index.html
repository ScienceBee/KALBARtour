DANAU SENTARUM

Danau Sentarum is one of the largest lakes in West Kalimantan, Indonesia. Located in Kapuas Hulu Regency, the lake is surrounded by extensive mangrove forests and is part of the Sentarum Lake National Park. Danau Sentarum spans approximately 7,000 hectares and consists of numerous small islands. Danau Sentarum is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty. Its clear and calm waters reflect the blue sky and the surrounding scenery. The lake is also a habitat for various rare and protected flora and fauna species, including different bird species, reptiles, mammals, and fish. Some of the rare species found in the lake include the water deer and various types of monkeys..

Lemukutan Island

Lemukutan Island is an island located administratively in the Sungai Raya Kepulauan District, Bengkayang Regency, West Kalimantan Province. I began my journey to Lemukutan Island from Pontianak, traveling by car to the ferry pier, which is approximately 130 km away with a travel time of around three and a half hours. Upon arrival at the pier, simple wooden boats can be seen used to transport people and goods to and from Lemukutan Island.

The island is a popular destination for travelers seeking a laid-back tropical getaway and a haven for beach lovers, divers, and snorkelers.

Merasap Waterfall

Merasap Waterfall is a notable waterfall located in West Kalimantan, Indonesia. It is situated in the Bengkayang Regency, specifically in the Sanggau District. The waterfall is renowned for its stunning natural beauty and is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

The beach is a favorite among locals and tourists alike due to its natural beauty and excellent conditions for swimming and surfing.

Tanjung Bajau

Tanjung Bajau is a cape located in West Kalimantan, Indonesia. It is situated in the Singkawang Regency, specifically in the North Singkawang District. Tanjung Bajau is renowned for its breathtaking natural scenery. Tanjung Bajau features a beautiful coastline with soft white sand and crystal-clear waters. From the cape, visitors can enjoy a wide panorama of the sea, with scattered small islands surrounding it. The sunset at Tanjung Bajau is a highly anticipated moment, where the sky is adorned with beautiful colors, creating a spectacular view.

Bawang Mountain

Gunung Bawang is a majestic mountain located in the province of West Kalimantan, Indonesia. It is part of the Schwaner Mountains, a range known for its rich biodiversity and stunning natural landscapes. Gunung Bawang stands tall at an elevation of approximately 1,850 meters above sea level, making it one of the prominent peaks in the region.

Sepancong Hill

Bukit Sepancong is a scenic hill located in the regency of Bengkayang, West Kalimantan, Indonesia. It is renowned for its natural beauty, panoramic views, and tranquil surroundings. The hill offers a peaceful retreat for nature lovers and those seeking a serene escape.

Covered in lush greenery and dotted with tall trees, Bukit Sepancong provides a refreshing atmosphere and a respite from the hustle and bustle of city life. Its elevated position allows visitors to enjoy breathtaking vistas of the surrounding landscape, including rolling hills, dense forests, and picturesque valleys.

Samudra Indah

Samudra Indah is a captivating beach located in the city of Singkawang, West Kalimantan, Indonesia. It is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike, offering a stunning coastline, clear blue waters, and a range of recreational activities.

The beach is known for its natural beauty, with long stretches of soft white sand and the tranquil waters of the Java Sea. Visitors can relax on the beach, sunbathe, and enjoy the gentle ocean breeze. The calm and serene atmosphere makes it an ideal spot for unwinding and escaping from the hustle and bustle of daily life.
